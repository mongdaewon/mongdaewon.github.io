**Where Is My Cursor - Mouse Highlighter**

Last updated: May 19, 2026

*This Privacy Policy is provided in English only.*

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Where Is My Cursor ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information when you use our macOS application.

## Information We Collect

Where Is My Cursor does **not** collect any data.

- The app has no network connectivity and never sends any information off your device.
- No analytics, tracking, telemetry, or crash reporting.
- No account, login, or registration is required.

## Local Data

Your preferences (such as colors, sizes, and shortcut options) are stored only on your device using the standard macOS preferences system. This data is never transmitted anywhere and is removed when you uninstall the app.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Photos, videos, screen contents, or files
- Contacts or calendar data
- Usage or analytics data

## Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics, or advertising.

## Advertising

Where Is My Cursor does not display any advertisements. We do not use any advertising SDKs.

## In-App Purchases

Where Is My Cursor does not offer any in-app purchases. The app is completely free.

## Data Security

All app preferences are stored locally on your device. We operate no servers and have no access to any of your data.

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Your Rights

- **Delete local data:** Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored preferences.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
